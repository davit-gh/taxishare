{% trans "How it works" %}

{% trans "You are undoubtedly familiar with the situation when you have to take a 'marshrutka' when it doesn't really have a free seat. You have to get to your work, to your studies or some other place and you cannot miss the 'marshrutka' otherwise you'll be late. Ordering taxi every day is pretty expensive and you have to take your ride standing all the way to your destination which is so annoying. You don't have to do that anymore, you can share the cost of your ride using 'Taxin Miasin'. The taxi fare will be divided among 3 passengers. You will enjoy your convenient ride and pay about 3 times less than ordering an ordinary taxi. It's a little more than a 'marshrutka' fare, it's just 350AMD per personâ€¤" %}

{% trans "Step 1" %}

{% trans "You need to enter your first and last names, username and mobile phone number for registration. You will receive SMS pin code you need to input to complete the registration. After registration you'll get an email with a link you need to click to activate your account." %}

{% trans "Step 2" %}

{% trans "Enter your route's starting and ending points, departure date/time, frequency and the number of passengers. As soon as 2 other passengers choose a similar route and date/time as you you'll get SMS confirmation with the details of your order. If you don't want to share the ride with 2 other passengers you can pay for 3 and have the taxi serve only you." %}

{% trans "Step 3" %}

{% trans "On the set date and time the taxi will be available in your specified starting point. You will identify the taxi according to the details in SMS like car model, licence plate. Just show the driver your order number and you're good to go!" %}

{% trans "Payment" %}

{% trans "Topping up your account is done via Paypal for now. We have plans to integrate more payment solutions like TelCell terminals and others in the future. We use https protocol so your communication with the website is completely safe and secure. After registration you have the option to top up your account and then create an order if you have enough credit on your account. Orders can be created starting from the next day of the current day. A single order costs 350AMD. One car will serve 3 persons." %}
