{% trans "How it works" %}

{% trans "You are undoubtedly familiar with the situation when you have to take a 'marshrutka' when it doesn't really have a free seat. You have to get to your work, to your studies or some other place and you cannot miss the 'marshrutka' otherwise you'll be late. Ordering taxi every day is pretty expensive and you have to take your ride standing all the way to your destination which is so annoying. You don't have to do that anymore, you can share the cost of your ride using 'Taxin Miasin'. The taxi fare, depending on the distance, will be divided among 3 passengers. You will enjoy your convenient ride and pay 3 times less than when you order an ordinary taxi." %}

{% trans "Step 1" %}

{% trans "Registration is very fast. It takes less than a minute. You need to enter your first and last names, username and mobile phone number for registration. You'll get an email with a link you need to click to activate your account." %}

{% trans "Step 2" %}

{% trans "Enter your route's starting and ending points, departure date/time, frequency and the number of passengers. As soon as 2 other passengers choose a similar route and date/time as you you'll get a phone call from us about the details of your order." %}

{% trans "Step 3" %}

{% trans "On the set date and time the taxi will be available in your specified starting point. You will identify the taxi according to the details we give you by phone. The car will stop by 2 other passengers along the way and take all 3 of you to your destinations!" %}

{% trans "Payment" %}

{% trans "You make the payment to the driver. You pay one third of the total cost. We handle the orders so that the difference between the routes of all 3 passengers is not significant so that it feels fair for everyone to divide the cost into 3 equal amounts. Orders can be created starting from the next day of the current day. One car will serve 3 persons because we think it is more comfortable that way." %}

{% trans "About us" %}

{% trans "We are a small group of active young people. We want to have our little contribution in making positive changes in our society. Hopefully 'Taxin Miasin' will be loved by our compatriotes and guests making their daily transportation more convenient and affordable. The idea of creating the website popped up out of our own gloomy experience when getting to work and back home was literally pain in a back. We all have a new choice now." %}
